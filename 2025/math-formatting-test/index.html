Math Formatting Test

Apr 12, 2025  
  • math
  • test

This is a test post for checking that mathematics formatting is working correctly.

Inline Math Examples

Here is an inline equation: \(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\). This is the Pythagorean theorem.

The quadratic formula is \(x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}\).

Einstein’s famous equation is \(E = mc^2\).

Block Math Examples

The Pythagorean theorem can also be written as:

\[ a^2 + b^2 = c^2 \]

Here’s a more complex equation using the block format:

\[ \begin{aligned} \nabla \times \vec{\mathbf{B}} -\, \frac1c\, \frac{\partial\vec{\mathbf{E}}}{\partial t} & = \frac{4\pi}{c}\vec{\mathbf{j}} \\ \nabla \cdot \vec{\mathbf{E}} & = 4 \pi \rho \\ \nabla \times \vec{\mathbf{E}}\, +\, \frac1c\, \frac{\partial\vec{\mathbf{B}}}{\partial t} & = \vec{\mathbf{0}} \\ \nabla \cdot \vec{\mathbf{B}} & = 0 \end{aligned} \]

Another way to display block math is using dollar signs:

$ f(x) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \hat{f}(\xi) e^{2\pi i \xi x} d\xi $

Matrices and Other Mathematical Structures

Here’s a matrix:

\[ \begin{pmatrix} a & b & c \\ d & e & f \\ g & h & i \end{pmatrix} \]

And a determinant:

\[ \begin{vmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{vmatrix} = ad - bc \]

Statistical Formulas

The normal distribution probability density function:

\[ f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}\right)^2} \]

Bayes’ theorem:

\[ P(A|B) = \frac{P(B|A)P(A)}{P(B)} \]

Complex Examples with Alignment

Kullback-Leibler divergence and Jensen-Shannon divergence:

\[ \begin{aligned} KL(\hat{y} || y) &= \sum_{c=1}^{M}\hat{y}_c \log{\frac{\hat{y}_c}{y_c}} \\ JS(\hat{y} || y) &= \frac{1}{2}(KL(y||\frac{y+\hat{y}}{2}) + KL(\hat{y}||\frac{y+\hat{y}}{2})) \end{aligned} \]

Chemical Equations

A simple chemical equation:

\[ \ce{H2O + CO2 -> H2CO3} \]
